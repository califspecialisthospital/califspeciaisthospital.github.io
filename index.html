Welcome to Your ealth Center

Calif Specialist Hospital is a Nigerian private hospital which specializes in Obstetrics, Gynaecology and Laboratory.

The Chief Medical Director is a Consultant Obstetrician and Gynecologist, winning many awards and being a member of numerous medical fellowships, with over 30 years of experience as a medical doctor and 10 years as a Consultant.

Services

Health Care Solutions

Obstetrics Services

We provide antenatal care, preconception care, postnatal care, and caesarean sections and manyothers.

Gynaecological Services

We offer gynecological surgery, contraception and family planning, as well as cervical cancer screening and many others.

General Surgery

We perform appendectomy, herniorrhaphy, bowel resection and anastomosis, among other procedures.

Ultrasound

We provide gynaecological ultrasound and obstetric ultrasound.

Laboratory

We provide a variety of laboratory tests, including the MP, Widal function test, urinalysis, full blood count with differentials, liver function tests, and many others to support accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Auxiliary Nursing Training

We also offer auxiliary nursing training for talented individuals at an affordable price. To register, please book an appointment and visit our hospital for further details.
